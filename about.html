Good Food, strong Roots About Our Restaurant

Founded in 2008 by the Mercy Musembi, our restaurant has been making dishes with the best fresh organic ingredients.We aim to please and ensure that our customers get the best service.Over the years we have manage to establish our restaurant in six different locations namely:Ruaka,Muthaiga,Karen,Kitusuru,Westlands and Milimani. Hawavi REstaurant is a 5-star Restaurant and was named the best Restaurant in 2022 and 2020 by HOME Magazine.

We guarantee that you will fall in lust with our decadent blends the moment you walk inside until you finish your last sip. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
